Why It Matters
Concrete shows every year
you skip cleaning it.

Concrete is porous — it absorbs everything. Oil, algae, mold, rust, and Amarillo's hard mineral-rich water all leave stains that get harder to remove the longer they sit. What looks like discoloration is actually biological growth and embedded contamination working its way deeper into the surface over time.

Professional concrete cleaning uses the right combination of pressure, technique, and pre-treatment solution to lift what regular hosing or DIY pressure washing leaves behind. We assess each surface and use the appropriate pressure — high traffic areas like driveways get more aggressive treatment, while decorative concrete and pool decks get careful lower-pressure cleaning to preserve the finish.

Clean concrete also lasts longer. Biological growth and embedded grime accelerate surface degradation. Regular cleaning is maintenance, not just cosmetic improvement.
